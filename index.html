About Joyania

Joyania is a peaceful, medieval-themed Minecraft server built on Christian values. We welcome players of all ages to explore, build, and adventure in a safe and uplifting environment. Whether you're constructing castles, farming in the countryside, or joining community worship events, Joyania is a place where faith and creativity thrive together.

Server Rules

  • Be respectful and kind to others.
  • No griefing, stealing, or trolling.
  • Keep language clean and family-friendly.
  • No inappropriate builds or behavior.
  • Honor God in your actions and words.
